LETTER FROM THE SECRETARY GENERAL

Distinguished Delegates,

It is with great pleasure that I welcome you to TFLMUN’25. As the Secretary General of this unique conference, I am deeply honored to announce that you are going to be a part of an event that promises to be both enriching and inspiring.

Over the past several months, our dedicated Secretariat and enthusiastic Organization Team have worked tirelessly to prepare an unforgettable experience for everyone. Every detail has been carefully planned with your growth and development in mind. Now, we are ready, and we eagerly await your participation.

During TFLMUN, you will have the opportunity to engage in thought-provoking debates, tackle global diplomatic issues, and challenge yourself intellectually. Through collaboration and discussion, you will enhance your understanding of international relations, develop your communication skills, and connect with a diverse group of bright and motivated individuals.

This conference is not just a platform for debate, but also a place where new perspectives are formed and meaningful connections are made. We believe that TFLMUN will not only be an academic experience but also a journey of personal growth.

We look forward to welcoming you and witnessing the valuable contributions you will bring to this event. Let this conference be the start of lasting friendships, fresh insights, and significant achievements.

Warm regards,
Secretary General of TFLMUN’25
İrem YENİGÜN

LETTER FROM THE DIRECTOR GENERAL

Distinguished Delegates,

It is with great honor and enthusiasm that I welcome you to TFLMUN’25 as the Director General.

Our organization team and I have worked tirelessly to create an environment where your ideas can develop, your voice can be heard, and your potential as future leaders can be realized.As Director General, I have ensured that every aspect of this conference has been meticulously planned to provide you with a meaningful and enriching experience.

In TFLMUN’25, the discussions you will engage in, the perspectives you will be exposed to, and the solutions you will craft are all part of a greater mission: shaping a generation that is not only aware of global issues but also ready to address them with vision and determination.

Our world today is facing challenges of increasing complexity. From political instability to environmental crises, from human rights violations to economic disparities, these are not distant concerns but realities that demand action. In conferences like this, awareness transforms into responsibility, and responsibility into action, thanks to your debates.

In contrast, TFLMUN is not just about debate; it is about building connections, gaining confidence, and becoming a part of a community that values your presence and cooperation. Since we are already on this topic, it is important to highlight another aspect.

The applications for our press team, which allows our conference to extend beyond these three days, are now open. We are looking forward to your contributions, ensuring that TFLMUN remains more than just unique memories.

Organizing this exceptional gathering has been an incredible journey. I am beyond eager to witness the unique perspectives, dedication, and passion that each of you will bring to TFLMUN’25.

Best regards,
Irmak Gök
Director General
