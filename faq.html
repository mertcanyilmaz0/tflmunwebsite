FAQ

What is TFLMUN? ▼
TFLMUN is the Model United Nations club of Tekirdağ Science High School.
How can I join? ▼
You can join by registering through our official website or contacting a club member.
When and where will the conference be held? ▼
The conference will be held on May 2-4 at Tekirdağ Namık Kemal University.
How can I apply? ▼
Delegates, delegations and press members can apply through the forms available on our website.
Can first-time participants apply? ▼
Yes, they can. We look forward to creating an unforgettable experience for you..
What is the official procedure of the conference? ▼
The conference will follow the Harvard Procedure.
What happens if delegates miss any sessions? ▼
Delegates who miss more than two official sessions of the conference unfortunately will not receive a certificate of participation.
Is there a dress code? ▼
Yes, there is. The dress code is Western Business Attire. For men, this typically includes a suit, dress shirt, tie, and dress shoes. For women, it includes a business suit (pants or skirt), blouse, and closed-toe shoes.
Will accommodation be provided? ▼
Yes, it will. Delegations from outside the city can stay at the Mills Hotel with at least one adult advisor. Reservations will be arranged by our logistics team. For further details, please contact us.
Will there be a social event? ▼
Yes, there will be. Further details will be announced soon.
