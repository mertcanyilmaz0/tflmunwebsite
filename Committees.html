COMMITTEES

  • Disarmament & International Security Committee(DISEC)

    Combating Terrorism in the Middle East and the Northern Africa

    Terrorism in these regions has posed a major threat to global peace and security. This committee will explore the root causes of terrorism, such as political instability and economic challenges, and discuss strategies to combat terrorist groups. Delegates will work together to offer solutions that enhance international cooperation, improve counterterrorism efforts and support regional stability.

  • The LEGAL Committee(LEGAL)

    The Role of International Law in Combating Modern Slavery and Human Trafficking

    Modern slavery and human trafficking affect millions of people worldwide, making international cooperation essential. This committee will explore existing global legal frameworks, such as UN conventions, and discuss ways to strengthen enforcement and protection mechanisms. Delegates will work together to offer solutions that improve victim support, enhance cross-border cooperation and ensure stronger punishments for offenders.

  • The United Nations Entity for Gender Equality and the Empowerment of Women(UNWOMEN)

    Eliminating Gender-Based Violence Linked to Traditional Gender Roles

    Gender-based violence remains a global issue, often rooted in harmful traditional gender norms. This committee will examine how cultural expectations and stereotypes contribute to violence and discrimination against women and marginalized groups. Delegates will work together to offer solutions that promote gender equality, raise awareness and strengthen legal protections.

  • United Nations Office on Drugs and Crime(UNODC)

    Addressing the Causes and Prevention of Juvenile Delinquency

    Crime tendency among youth is a growing concern worldwide, influenced by various socio-economic and psychological aspects. Unemployment, poverty, lack of education and family instability tends young people to criminal behavior. As world leaders and policymakers, delegates will work together and discuss solutions that support at-risk youth to address this issue effectively.
